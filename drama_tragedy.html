A Silent Voice 10 out of 10

An elementary school girl who is deaf, Shoko Nishimiya, transfers to a new school and meets a boy named Shoya Ishida. Shoya after finding out she's deaf has the whole class bully her. However, the class starts to bully Shoya for bullying Shoko. Shoko and Shoya do not speak to each other after elementary but Shoya grows up feeling guilty over what he has done and goes after Shoko for amends and tries to be her friend.
